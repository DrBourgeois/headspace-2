Project Title 2

Installation • 2024 • Mixed media, video, and code

This project explores the intersection of network protocols, surveillance, and speculative media. It was exhibited in Berlin in 2024 as part of an experimental art and technology showcase.

The installation involved custom hardware and a mesh networking system that created its own hidden internet inside the gallery. Visitors could connect and interact anonymously, but the system simultaneously monitored and distorted communication — raising questions about visibility, control, and resistance within digital infrastructures.
