Reflections on Noise

August 2025

I’ve been thinking about noise—digital noise, urban noise, mental noise. Sometimes it feels like a disturbance, other times like a hidden pattern waiting to be decoded. Maybe noise is just an invitation to listen differently.

“Noise is a signal misunderstood.” — anonymous hacker note I once scribbled.

Here’s a photo experiment where I intentionally pushed my camera into overexposure and grain. The result reminded me of corrupted data packets, like the way the internet frays at the edges.
