The Edges of Memory

Aug 18, 2025 — draft notes, unfinished, but alive

Today I stared at the reflection of my laptop screen on a café window. Two worlds overlapped — inside and outside — like how art exists in memory before it ever takes shape. Maybe this site is also a window. A repetition. A sketch.

I want this blog to hold fragments: unfinished ideas, cables scattered on the floor, sounds that collapse into silence once played. Less blog, more glitch diary.

“A blog is an archive of hesitations.”

This is only a placeholder. But maybe placeholders are the most honest state. Things mid-way, not yet solidified, not yet corrupted by polish.

Until next time,
– pepe
