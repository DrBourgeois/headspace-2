The Edges of Memory

August 18, 2025 · by Pepe

Today I found myself staring at the reflection of my screen on a café window. It was both inside and outside, a doubled presence—like the way art lives in my head before it ever takes form. I thought: maybe websites are the same. A window, a repetition, a place where nothing and everything gathers.

I’m building this small corner of the internet as a sketchbook, a vessel for unfinished thoughts, images I don’t know how to frame, and sounds that dissolve into silence once played.

“A blog is an archive of hesitations.”

For now, this is only a placeholder. But I want it to feel like sitting with me at the studio table, coffee stains on the paper, cables scattered across the floor, fragments of ideas becoming something slowly.

Until next time,
– Pepe
