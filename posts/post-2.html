The COAPPARATION I, II, III zine. The zine "COAPPARATION, I, II, II" presents projects addressed to thealit or realized at thealit in the COAPPARATION program 2019-2022.

a spread of my poetry and visual art was featured in thealit Bremen's publication of the zine COAPPARATION I, II, III (see it here: thealit.de). At the time, the work appeared under my former name (Müge Pelin Sen), but it remains an important marker in my creative path. The spread combines text and visuals in ways I still explore today, and it’s meaningful to see how those early experiments found a home in an independent publication.
